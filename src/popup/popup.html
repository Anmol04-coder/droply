Droply

Intructions:

1. Activate the Droply Extension.

2. Click around the page to see the color. All the buttons and links will be frozen at this point.

3. Copy the value in RGB, HEX or HSL.

4. Click "x" to close the tool and reactivate the links on the page. When you go to another page in the same tab, Droply will reopen. Unactivate the extension if you don't need it anymore.
